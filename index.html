ABOUT ISIGHT

ISIGHT is a triage and referral app for school counselors. It's age appropriate design makes it easy and safe for students to self-refer to a school counselor, and teachers and staff can use it to refer students as well. In many school systems, the student to counselor ratio is growing. ISIGHT helps the counselor connect the students who need help faster and more accurately.
